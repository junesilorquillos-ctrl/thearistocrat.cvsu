Our Heritage

A Legacy Born in 1936

Founded by Engracia "Lola Asiang" Cruz Reyes — revered as the Mother of Filipino Cooking — The Aristocrat has nourished generations of Filipino families with recipes built on love, tradition, and the finest local ingredients.

For nearly nine decades, our kitchen has been the keeper of flavors that feel like home: slow-cooked, soul-deep, and made to be shared around the table.

"Food is our love language — it brings the whole family together, across every generation."

— Lola Asiang, Founder
